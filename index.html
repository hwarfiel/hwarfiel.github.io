A SHORT BIO...

As the son of Missionaries I lived in six countries growing up including France, Mauritnia, and Senegal. During my 16+ years overseas I had many cultural expeiernces that broadened my understadning of th world.

🇫🇷 FRANCE

For a year I called Albertville, France my home. There I was enrolled in the French School System and learn to speak French.

🇺🇬 UGANDA

During my three years in Uganda I spent my time volunteering around Good Shepards Fold Orphanage, which served underprivilleged Ugandan kids. I also learned Swahili and Luganda languages while living the in the forest regions.

🇸🇳 SENEGAL

Dakar, Senegal became my home for Junior High and High School. There I was Ingrained with the culture of Senegal learning to make Attaya (traditional West-African tea), competed in soccer with locals, and particapted in community outreach programs several villages across the Terenga.

🇲🇷 MAURITANIA

During my formative years I lived in the slums of Nouakchott the capital of Mauritania. I lived without runnning water, basic sanitation, or a solid stool to sit on. There I saw poverty in its truest form.
